# MedTech Compliance Solutions LLC - Recommended Business Documents

## Executive Summary
This document outlines 8 essential business documents recommended for MedTech Compliance Solutions LLC to optimize operations, ensure regulatory compliance, and drive business growth. Each document serves a specific purpose in building a robust, scalable medical device software company.

---

## 1. Quality Management System (QMS) Manual

### Purpose
Comprehensive documentation of our quality management system, ensuring ISO 13485 compliance and demonstrating our commitment to quality in software development for medical devices.

### Key Sections
- **Quality Policy**: Company's commitment to quality and regulatory compliance
- **Organizational Structure**: Roles, responsibilities, and reporting relationships
- **Quality Objectives**: Measurable goals for quality improvement
- **Document Control**: Procedures for document creation, review, and approval
- **Risk Management**: Identification and mitigation of quality risks
- **Supplier Quality**: Requirements for third-party vendors and partners
- **Internal Audits**: Schedule and procedures for quality system audits
- **Management Review**: Process for evaluating QMS effectiveness

### Business Value
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Required for ISO 13485 certification
- **Customer Confidence**: Demonstrates quality commitment to medical device customers
- **Operational Efficiency**: Standardized processes reduce errors and improve consistency
- **Audit Preparation**: Streamlines regulatory audits and inspections

### Implementation Timeline
- **Phase 1**: Draft core sections (Months 1-2)
- **Phase 2**: Implement procedures (Months 3-6)
- **Phase 3**: ISO 13485 certification audit (Months 7-9)
