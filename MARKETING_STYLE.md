### Visual Identity

#### Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo**: MedTech Compliance Solutions wordmark with AI circuit icon
- **Icon Only**: For favicons, app icons, and small spaces
- **Minimum Size**: 24px for digital, 1" for print
- **Clear Space**: Minimum 1x logo height around logo

#### Color Palette

##### Primary Colors
- **Navy Blue** (#1e40af): Trust, professionalism, healthcare
  - RGB: 30, 64, 175
  - CMYK: 83, 63, 0, 31
  - Usage: Headlines, primary buttons, logos

- **Medical Teal** (#0d9488): Health, technology, innovation
  - RGB: 13, 148, 136
  - CMYK: 91, 0, 8, 42
  - Usage: Secondary elements, accents, links

##### Secondary Colors
- **Compliance Green** (#16a34a): Success, compliance, growth
  - RGB: 22, 163, 74
  - Usage: Success states, positive indicators

- **Alert Orange** (#ea580c): Attention, warnings, action
  - RGB: 234, 88, 12
  - Usage: CTAs, alerts, highlights

- **Risk Red** (#dc2626): Critical issues, errors
  - RGB: 220, 38, 38
  - Usage: Error states, critical alerts

##### Neutral Colors
- **Charcoal** (#374151): Primary text, professional tone
- **Slate** (#64748b): Secondary text, muted elements
- **Cloud** (#f8fafc): Backgrounds, clean spaces
- **White** (#ffffff): Primary backgrounds, contrast

#### Typography

##### Primary Typeface: Inter
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)
- **Usage**: All digital communications, web interfaces
- **Why**: Modern, highly legible, excellent for UI/UX

##### Secondary Typeface: Source Serif Pro
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Semibold (600)
- **Usage**: Print materials, formal documents, presentations
- **Why**: Professional, authoritative, excellent readability

##### Type Scale
- **H1**: 2.25rem (36px) / Bold / Navy Blue
- **H2**: 1.875rem (30px) / Semibold / Navy Blue
- **H3**: 1.5rem (24px) / Semibold / Navy Blue
- **H4**: 1.25rem (20px) / Medium / Navy Blue
- **Body Large**: 1.125rem (18px) / Regular / Charcoal
- **Body**: 1rem (16px) / Regular / Charcoal
- **Body Small**: 0.875rem (14px) / Regular / Slate
- **Caption**: 0.75rem (12px) / Regular / Slate

### Voice & Tone

#### Brand Voice
- **Professional**: Expert, authoritative, trustworthy
- **Innovative**: Forward-thinking, cutting-edge, visionary
- **Approachable**: Helpful, collaborative, human-centered
- **Confident**: Assured, capable, reliable

#### Tone Guidelines

##### Formal Communications (White Papers, Proposals)
- Use complete sentences and proper grammar
- Maintain professional distance
- Focus on facts, data, and expertise
- Example: "Our AI-powered platform ensures 100% compliance with ISO 13485 standards."

##### Marketing Communications (Website, Emails)
- Conversational yet professional
- Focus on benefits and outcomes
- Use active voice
- Example: "Transform your compliance process with intelligent automation."

##### Social Media & Casual Communications
- Friendly and approachable
- Industry-specific language appropriate
- Engage with community
- Example: "Excited to share how AI is revolutionizing medical device compliance! #MedTech #Compliance"

##### Crisis Communications
- Empathetic and transparent
- Focus on resolution and prevention
- Maintain confidence in solutions
- Example: "We take data security seriously. Here's how we're addressing this issue."
