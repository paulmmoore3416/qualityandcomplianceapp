## Core Business Model

### 1. **Software as a Service (SaaS) Platform**
- **Primary Revenue Stream**: Subscription-based access to our compliance platform
- **Pricing Tiers**:
  - **Starter**: $2,500/month (up to 50 users, basic features)
  - **Professional**: $7,500/month (up to 200 users, advanced analytics)
  - **Enterprise**: $15,000+/month (unlimited users, custom integrations)
- **Deployment Options**: Cloud-hosted or on-premises

### 2. **Professional Services**
- **Implementation Services**: $50,000 - $150,000 per project
- **Custom Development**: $150/hour
- **Training & Support**: $2,500/day for on-site training
- **Compliance Consulting**: $300/hour

### 3. **Annual Maintenance & Support**
- **Gold Support**: 15% of license fee annually
- **Platinum Support**: 25% of license fee annually (24/7 support, 1-hour response)
